Mandelbrot Fractal Visualisations

November 2023

  • Variations 1, 4, 5, & 6: an enlarged section of variation 3.
  • Variation 2: the classic Mandelbrot fractal, with some efforts made to smooth the colour gradient. I originally used a tutorial by Karl Sims, although unfortunately it seems that it is no longer publically available.
  • Variation 3: a Mandelbrot-esque escape time fractal of my own creation (which I have dubbed the "frog fractal" due to its close resemblance to a frog) using the recurrence relation: z = zz + c × zz
